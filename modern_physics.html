Modern Physics Formulas

Quantum Physics

De Broglie Wavelength: \( \lambda = \frac{h}{p} \)

  • \( \lambda \) = wavelength
  • \( h \) = Planck’s constant
  • \( p \) = momentum

Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle: \( \Delta x \Delta p \geq \frac{\hbar}{2} \)

  • \( \Delta x \) = uncertainty in position
  • \( \Delta p \) = uncertainty in momentum
  • \( \hbar \) = reduced Planck’s constant (\( \frac{h}{2\pi} \))

Nuclear Physics

Mass-Energy Equivalence: \( E = mc^2 \)

  • \( E \) = energy
  • \( m \) = mass
  • \( c \) = speed of light

Binding Energy per Nucleon: \( E_b = \frac{\Delta m \cdot c^2}{A} \)

  • \( E_b \) = binding energy per nucleon
  • \( \Delta m \) = mass defect
  • \( A \) = mass number
