Oscillations, Waves, and Light Formulas

Oscillations and Waves - Waves - Light

Oscillations and Waves

Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM) - Position: \( x(t) = A \cos(\omega t + \phi) \)

  • \( A \) = amplitude
  • \( \omega \) = angular frequency
  • \( t \) = time
  • \( \phi \) = phase constant

Angular Frequency: \( \omega = 2 \pi f \)

  • \( f \) = frequency

Period: \( T = \frac{1}{f} = \frac{2\pi}{\omega} \)

  • \( T \) = period

Hooke's Law: \( F = -kx \)

  • \( F \) = restoring force
  • \( k \) = spring constant
  • \( x \) = displacement

Energy in SHM: \( E = \frac{1}{2} k A^2 \)

Waves

Wave Speed: \( v = f \lambda \)

  • \( v \) = wave speed
  • \( f \) = frequency
  • \( \lambda \) = wavelength

Wave Equation: \( y(x,t) = A \sin(kx - \omega t + \phi) \)

  • \( y(x,t) \) = wave function
  • \( A \) = amplitude
  • \( k \) = wave number (\( k = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \))
  • \( \omega \) = angular frequency (\( \omega = 2\pi f \))
  • \( t \) = time
  • \( \phi \) = phase constant

Intensity: \( I = \frac{P}{A} \)

  • \( I \) = intensity
  • \( P \) = power
  • \( A \) = area

Light

Speed of Light: \( c = 3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s} \)

Index of Refraction: \( n = \frac{c}{v} \)

  • \( n \) = index of refraction
  • \( c \) = speed of light in vacuum
  • \( v \) = speed of light in the medium

Snell's Law: \( n_1 \sin(\theta_1) = n_2 \sin(\theta_2) \)

  • \( n_1 \) = index of refraction of medium 1
  • \( \theta_1 \) = angle of incidence
  • \( n_2 \) = index of refraction of medium 2
  • \( \theta_2 \) = angle of refraction

Lens Maker's Equation: \( \frac{1}{f} = (n - 1) \left( \frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2} \right) \)

  • \( f \) = focal length
  • \( n \) = index of refraction of the lens material
  • \( R_1 \) = radius of curvature of the first lens surface
  • \( R_2 \) = radius of curvature of the second lens surface

Energy of a Photon: \( E = hf \)

  • \( E \) = energy
  • \( h \) = Planck's constant (\( 6.626 \times 10^{-34} \, \text{J s} \))
  • \( f \) = frequency
